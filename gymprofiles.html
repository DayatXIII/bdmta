Gym's Profile

RENEGADE Martial Arts Academy and Fitness was established with the purpose of providing a training ground where one can learn the art of combat, have fun in the experience, make a commitment to that learning and to use the knowledge as a “stepping stone” to an improved self, family and community. Our Academy offers a variety of classes to accommodate students of all ages and skill levels which include Muay Thai, Boxing and basic self-defense. For fitness, the centre offers programmes for Strength & conditioning as well as weight loss. Our instructors are either currently active fighters or are certified and either have the knowledge and experience to teach a large group class.
